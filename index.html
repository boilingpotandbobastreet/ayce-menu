All You Can Eat Hot Pot Menu

AYCE Rules

  1. AYCE is limited to 90 minutes per table.
  2. All guests at the table must choose the same AYCE level: Regular or Premium.
  3. Guests will be charged by the stated size of the party.
  4. A 15% gratuity will be added to parties of 6 or more. For gratuity payments, only one payment type may be used.
  5. Any leftovers will incur a $7 additional charge per person.
  6. No take-out/to-go boxes can be provided for leftover AYCE food.
  7. Dinner pricing applies all day on weekends, Saturday/Sunday, and holidays.
  8. A maximum of 2 credit cards may be used per table.
